Who We Are

We are disciples of Christ, empowered by His resurrection. We gather to share the Good News, embodying love and faith, inspiring hope, and serving others as witness of His transformative grace.

What We Believe

We believe in the Trinity: one God in three persons- Father, Son, Holy Spirit. Each co-equal and co-eternal, united essence, purpose, and love, guiding us in faith and worship.

Fuel Our Mission

Join us in making a difference! Your generosity helps support our mission, uplifting our community and spreading love. Every contribution, big or small, empowers us to serve and transform lives. Thank you!
