Immanuel Methodist United Church was established in 2018 to support the Myanmar refugee community resett in the United States. Founded by individuals escaping conflict and persecution in Myanmar, the church serves as a spiritual haven that fosters unity and cultural preservation. It provides worship services, fellowship opportunities, and essential resources to help members navigate their new lives. Through its congregation, Immanuel Methodist United Church embodies hope and resilience, creating a nurturing environment where refugees can thrive together in faith.

Our Vision:

To empower believers to boldly proclaim the resurrection of Christ, transforming lives through the Good News. We envision a world where faith ignites hope and inspires action in every community.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to share the message of Jesus Christ's resurrection with all nations. Through love, service, and education, we strive to equip disciples and spread hope, faith, and transformative grace..

Our Goal:

Our goal is to reach every person with the Gospel by fostering discipleship, building community, and encouraging outreach initiatives, ensuring that the message of Christ’s resurrection inspires change throughout the world..
